Safety Rules
No fuses = fire risk (NEC 690.9)
Ground frames over 50V (NEC 690.41)
Disconnect before maintenance (NEC 690.13)
Reverse polarity = equipment damage
Undersized wire overheats
PWM = parallel panels only
MPPT = series or parallel panels
LiFePO4 requires MPPT controller
Lead-Acid requires vented enclosure
AFCI required on building-mounted systems (NEC 690.11)
Rapid shutdown required on rooftop systems (NEC 690.12)
Label all disconnects (NEC 690.17)
